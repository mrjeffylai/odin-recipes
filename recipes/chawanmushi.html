How to Make Chawanmushi!

Description

For the warmest and welcoming appetizer to grace a Japanese meal, it always feels right to start with steaming cups of chawanmushi. Made with dashi-infused egg mixture with the freshest seasonal ingredients, this Japanese savory egg custard is meltingly smooth and deeply flavored.

This is great way to make your morning egg dish less calorically dense (probably) because your adding a buncha water and stuff to it!

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1.75 cups of dashi broth
  • 1 tablespoon of mirin
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons of salt

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs.
  2. Dump all them ingredients into the eggs.
  3. Beat everything together.
  4. Strain into a bowl.
  5. Cover the bowl.
  6. Add 1 cup water to InstantPot.
  7. Put bowl of egg into InstantPot and pressure cook for 3 minutes.
  8. Eat it.
