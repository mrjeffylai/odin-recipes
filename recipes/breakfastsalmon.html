Mmmm Breakfast Salmon!

Description

Japanese people sometimes eat breakfast for salmon. Wait...

Anyway, it seems pretty healthy compared to waffles and French Toast.

Ingredients

  • 1 piece of lightly salted salmon
  • Some oil

Steps

  1. Oil up the pan.
  2. Heat up pan on medium heat.
  3. Place salmon upon thy pan.
  4. Cook one side for 1 minute.
  5. Cook the other side for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Eat it.
